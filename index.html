We create tattoos of all types

01

Realism

Uses special techniques to make tattoos realistic. Shading and contrast are prioritized. It is possible to fade in 8-10 years but with a little touch up it will be perfect again

02

Thrash Polka

Thrash Polka was invented in Germany. They usually tell a story and the design are chaotic. The designs are most often abstract with elements of realism. They are confusing but very beautiful

03

Japanese aka Irezumi

Very traditional style of tattooing using large scale motifs based on Japanese mythology and religious icons. It has a very rich history behind it. Back in the days they were synonymous to criminals and yakuza, but today is one of the most popular styles out there

04

Traditional American Tattoos

They have bold lines and bright solid colors it should be a good size and easy to understand with minimal shading, focusing on black as a main color and other help colors
